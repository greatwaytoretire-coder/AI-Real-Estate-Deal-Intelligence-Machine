# PHASE 5 — PROPERTY INTELLIGENCE AND OPPORTUNITY SCORING

## Goal

Establish the initial explainable opportunity scoring foundation for the AI Real Estate Deal Intelligence Machine.

## Scope

This phase introduces a bounded opportunity intelligence layer that can:

- evaluate qualified properties across multiple signals,
- calculate multiple explainable scores,
- produce an explainable scorecard,
- promote high-scoring opportunities into a priority queue.

## Scores Included

- Opportunity Score
- Deal Potential Score
- Market Score
- Buyer Demand Score
- Risk Score
- Data Confidence Score
- Urgency Score

## Constraints

- This phase does not implement full underwriting or buyer outreach.
- The scoring engine is intentionally local and explainable.
- The queue promotion behavior is a foundation only and is not connected to live workflows yet.
